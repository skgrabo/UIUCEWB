Join Us

Thank you for your interest in EWB-UIUC. We are glad to have you!

No engineering experience? Don’t worry! We welcome everyone regardless of your major and level of experience.

Simply follow these steps to join us:

  • Join our Discord!
  • Subscribe to our weekly EWB newsletter!
  • Follow our Instagram @ewb.uiuc or our Facebook page @EWBUIUC!
  • Attend any of our project meetings. Feel free to join us at any point of time – whether it be at the start, middle or even end of the semester!

Please reach out to ask on Discord if you have any questions. We are excited to have you on our team!
